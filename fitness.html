Principles

💧
Hydration
Drink at least half your body weight in ounces of water daily. Add 8oz per 20 minutes of intense activity.
😴
Recovery
Muscles grow during rest, not during the workout. Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep and honor your rest days.
🥩
Nutrition
Target 0.7–1g of protein per pound of bodyweight daily. Prioritize protein at each meal, don't skip breakfast.
📈
Progressive Overload
Add a small amount of weight or reps each week. Even 2.5lbs per session adds up to 130lbs in a year.
🧘
Warm Up
Spend 5–10 minutes warming up before every session. Dynamic stretches and mobility work prevent injury.
📓
Track Everything
Log your weights, reps, and how you felt. What gets measured gets improved. Consistency leads to results.
